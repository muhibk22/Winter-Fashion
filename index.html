What are denim jackets made of?

Denim is a sturdy cotton fabric crafted with a twill weave, resulting in a distinct diagonal ribbing pattern. This type of cotton twill fabric is warp-facing, indicating that the weft threads pass under two or more warp threads. As a result, the warp yarns are more visible on the fabric's right side.
